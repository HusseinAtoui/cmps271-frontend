SUBMIT TO: dakota@nowheregirlcollective.com

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:

Submissions are open from 13-16th of each month. Any submissions sent outside of 13-16th will be automatically disregarded.

1 submission per person, per prompt. Submissions must be based on the prompt.

Please use the following format for the email subject line: SUBMISSION: PROMPT, GENRE (essay/poetry/fiction/art), TITLE OF WORK

ESSAY:

Max 2000 words per submission. Include title, word count, brief pitch, and citations (if any).

POETRY:

1 poem per submission. Include title and format properly.

FICTION:

Max 2000 words per submission. You may submit a short story, vignette, or excerpt.

ART:

Attach images or videos. Include title and medium.
