General Submission Guidelines

Afterthoughts is an English journal published in print annually. The call for submissions for our print edition is announced on social media and this website. All submissions will be considered for print and online publication. To submit, see our Submittable portal for open calls.

If you have questions, please send an e-mail to info@rustedradishes.com. Any work submitted not in line with these guidelines will not be accepted or read.

Submission Requirements

  • Attachment: Each writing piece should be attached in a separate .doc file. Each art piece should be sent as a low-res attachment.
  • Bio: Provide a brief bio (third-person, max 80 words). Indicate whether you are a student, faculty, or alum of AUB, or your connection to Lebanon or the region.
  • Formatting: Use a 12-pt font and double-space prose. Keep poetry formatting intact.
  • Multiple Submissions: Check specific genre guidelines for limits.
  • File Naming: Use format ‘title_genre’. Include your full name and e-mail at the top of the document.
  • Simultaneous Submissions: These are allowed. Withdraw work by emailing submit@rustedradishes.com or using Submittable.
